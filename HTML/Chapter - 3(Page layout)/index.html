The SECTION Tag

The <section> tag groups related content together, typically under a heading.

The ARTICLE Tag

The <article> tag represents independent content such as blog posts, news articles, or user-generated content.

The ASIDE Tag

The <aside> tag contains supplementary content like sidebars, ads, or related links.
