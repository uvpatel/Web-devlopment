Chapter-5: Video, Iframes, and Audio Tags

Video Tag:

Video tag's attributes:

  • src: Specifies the URL of the video file.
  • width: Adjusts the width of the video player.
  • preload: Specifies if the video should be preloaded.
  • controls: Displays video controls.
  • loop: Restarts video automatically.
  • autoplay: Starts video when page loads.

Second syntax:

                <video width="640" autoplay loop>
                <source src="harry.mp4" type="video/mp4">
                Your browser does not support the video tag.
                </video>

Audio Tag:

Audio tag's attributes:

  • src: Specifies the URL of the audio file.
  • controls: Displays audio controls.
  • autoplay: Automatically plays audio.
  • loop: Repeats audio when finished.
  • muted: Starts the audio in muted state.
  • preload: Defines how the audio loads.

Iframe Tag:

Iframe is used to embed external content, such as videos, maps, and other websites.

Example: Embedding YouTube Video

Iframe attributes:

  • src: Specifies the URL to be embedded.
  • width & height: Defines the size of the iframe.
  • frameborder: Sets border (0 for none).
  • allowfullscreen: Enables fullscreen mode.
  • sandbox: Adds security restrictions.
